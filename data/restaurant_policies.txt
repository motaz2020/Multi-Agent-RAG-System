# Restaurant Policies

## Opening Hours
- Monday to Thursday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Friday to Saturday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM
- The restaurant is closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day.

## Reservations
- Reservations can be made online or by phone.
- Reservations are recommended for parties of 4 or more.
- A credit card is required to secure reservations for parties of 6 or more.
- Reservations can be modified up to 2 hours before the booking time.
- Cancellations must be made at least 4 hours in advance.
- Late arrivals of more than 15 minutes may result in table release.
- Maximum party size per reservation is 12 guests.
- For parties larger than 12, please contact our events team.

## Refund Policy
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Full refund available for cancellations made more than 24 hours before the reservation.
- 50% refund for cancellations made between 4-24 hours before the reservation.
- No refund for cancellations made less than 4 hours before the reservation.
- Food quality complaints will be addressed by the manager on duty.
- Overcharged orders will be refunded the difference immediately.

## Birthday Events
- Birthday events can be arranged for parties of any size.
- Free dessert for the birthday person.
- Birthday decoration packages start at $25.
- Private room available for parties of 10 or more at no extra charge.
- Custom cake allowed with 48-hour advance notice.
- Special birthday menu available for groups of 8 or more.

## Catering Rules
- Catering services available for events of 20 or more guests.
- Orders must be placed at least 72 hours in advance.
- Delivery available within 10 miles of any branch.
- Delivery fee: $25 within 5 miles, $50 for 5-10 miles.
- Setup and cleanup included for orders over $500.
- Custom menu consultation available for events over $1000.
- Dietary restrictions can be accommodated with 48-hour notice.
- Payment required in full at time of order for catering.
